Facts About Dogs

Dogs have sense of time. It's been proven that they know the difference between a hour and a five. If conditioned to, they can predict future events, such as regular walk times.

A study was conducted on dogs being away from their owners for varying hours and the studies show that dogs who were away from their owner the longest shoed the greatest amount of affection!
